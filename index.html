NOVA Wall

-NOVA Wall is a sub-project under Break the Habit, Quit E-cigarette, which is the last activity for aspiring adolescents whose path is quitting smoking. This sub-program will serve as the visual oath of vape quitters by participating in a mural painting dedicated to showcasing their goals in their journey to vaping.

-This sub-program aims to have a mural that can be done by anyone as long as the theme revolves around quitting vaping.

-NOVA Wall will provide the vape quitters with the materials they need if they do not have any for the mural.
